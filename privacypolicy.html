Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/28/2024

At MentorPro, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services, including the MentorPro website and mobile app (the “Services”). By accessing or using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in a variety of ways, including through the use of our Services. This information helps us provide you with the best possible experience and improve our Services over time.

a. Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account with MentorPro, we collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you choose to provide to set up your profile.
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase or subscribe to any paid service, we may collect your payment details, such as credit card or bank account information, to process the transaction securely.

b. Usage Data

  • Log Data: We automatically collect log data, such as your device's IP address, browser type, pages viewed, time spent on the app, and other usage statistics. This data is used to enhance user experience and improve our Services.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the Services, including the device type, operating system, browser type, and device settings.

c. Location Data

If you enable location services on your device, we may collect your geographic location to provide location-based features, such as nearby mentors or mentees. You can disable location tracking at any time by adjusting your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for a variety of purposes, including:

  • To provide and personalize our Services and features.
  • To process your transactions and manage your account.
  • To communicate with you, including sending updates, promotional offers, and customer support messages.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our Services and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your personal information in the following ways:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party companies who provide services such as payment processing, customer support, and data analytics. These providers are obligated to protect your data and are prohibited from using it for any other purposes.
  • With Other Users: Depending on the features you use, some of your profile information (e.g., name, bio, profile photo) may be visible to other users. We do not share sensitive information like payment details or passwords with other users.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of MentorPro, our users, or others.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to secure your personal data, including using encryption and other security technologies. However, no method of transmitting or storing data is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

5. Your Choices and Rights

As a user, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information at any time through your account settings.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails or push notifications by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications.
  • Data Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and personal information. Some data may be retained for legal or business purposes, as required by law.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience with MentorPro. These technologies help us analyze usage patterns, remember your preferences, and improve our Services. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

MentorPro is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will post an updated version with a new "Effective Date."
